ABOUT


The Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival is a yearly film festival in order to unite all the movie lovers together. This year is the BOFF's 15th anniversary. Jennifer Viala and the City of Brooklyn are happy to annouce that every showing is going to be suitable for all ages thus enabling entire families to come and take in the great athmosphere, meet other interesting people and enjoy the free buffet offered by the City of Brooklyn. The BOFF team and organizers have worked on making the event generally bigger: The size of the screen had been doubled, the events maximum capacity has grown from 1,400 people to 5,000 people from one year to the next. We have also decided to increase the number of showings; this year there will be 4 movies per night for 4 nights summing up to 16 movies for you to discover. If you are interested in attending this event please reserve your film and time. The reservation and the showing are completely free of charge. The registration is only in order to have an idea of the number of spectators to prepare to correct amount of snacks and drinks to waste as little as possible.


 
 
 

DATES & LOCATION


All showings will be displayed in the beautiful Brooklyn Bridge Park everyday from the Saturday the 5th August to the Wednesday the 8th August from 6pm to midnight.
